【1】Hi!My name is Wang Ling.My English name is Lily.I'm from Hebei.I have a happy family.There are four people in my family，my father，my mother，my brother and me.My father is a teacher.He teaches Chinese in a school.He goes to school by car.My mother is a doctor.She works in a hospital.She walks to work.Because the hospital is not far from our home.My brother is a student.He goes to school by bike.My favourite school work is to read books.Books are my friends，very good friends.This is me，a happy girl.
嗨! 我叫王玲。我的英文名字是莉莉。我来自河北。我有一个幸福的家庭。我家有四口人，我的爸爸、妈妈、哥哥和我。我的爸爸是一名教师。他在一所学校教语文。他开车去学校。我的妈妈是一名医生。她在医院工作。她步行去上班。因为医院离我们家不远。我的哥哥是一名学生。他骑自行车去上学。我最喜欢的学校活动是读书。书是我的朋友,非常好的朋友。这是我,一个快乐的女孩。

teach Chinese（教中文）
