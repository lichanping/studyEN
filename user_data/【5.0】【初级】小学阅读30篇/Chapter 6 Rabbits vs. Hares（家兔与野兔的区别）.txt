【1】Do you know the differences between rabbits and hares ？Rabbits look like hares．They both have long ears and short tails．And they both eat grass．
【2】Rabbits are popular pets．They are gentle and friendly animals．When baby rabbits are born，they can't see anything at first and they have no hair．About eight days later，their eyes open and they begin to grow some hair．
【3】Hares are bigger than rabbits and have longer ears and legs．Hares can run very fast and jump high．They can run about 70 kilometres an hour．Baby hares are very active and they open their eyes soon after they are born．

家兔是受欢迎的宠物。它们是温顺友好的动物。小家兔出生时，一开始它们什么也看不见，而且没有毛发。大约八天后，它们睁开眼睛，开始长出一些毛发。
野兔比家兔大，耳朵和腿更长。野兔能跑得很快，跳得很高。它们每小时能跑70公里左右。小野兔非常活泼，出生后不久就睁开眼睛。
