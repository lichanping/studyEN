【1】The Zhang family drinks a lot of drinks every day.Early in the morning，grandparents like drinking a glass of water with salt after they get up.It's good for their bodies.For breakfast Mr Zhang always drinks a cup of tea.Mrs Zhang drinks a glass of water with honey.It's sweet and healthy.Their son Zhang Jun usually has a glass of orange juice.During lunch Zhang Jun doesn't drink anything at school.He drinks some water after lunch.On his way home he sometimes buys a can of coke or a bottle of juice.In the evening Zhang Jun always drinks a glass of warm milk before going to bed.It helps Zhang Jun sleep well at night.Mr Zhang usually goes to bed late.He works in the study in the evening.When he is tired he often drinks a cup of coffee.
张家的人每天喝很多饮品。一大早，爷爷奶奶起床后喜欢喝一杯淡盐水。这对他们的身体有好处。张先生早餐总是喝一杯茶。张太太喝一杯加蜂蜜的水。它又甜又健康。他们的儿子张军通常喝一杯橙汁。张军在学校吃午饭时不喝东西。他午饭后喝一些水。在回家的路上,他有时会买一罐可乐或一瓶果汁。晚上张军在睡觉前 总是喝杯热牛奶这有助于张军晚上睡个好觉。张先生通常很晚才睡觉。他晚上在书房工作。当他累了的时候，他经常喝一杯咖啡。

段落大意：张家成员每日不同时间的饮品习惯，包括盐水、茶、蜂蜜水、橙汁、可乐和牛奶等。
段落短语：

drink a glass of water with salt（喝一杯盐水）
drink a cup of tea（喝一杯茶）
drink a glass of water with honey（喝一杯蜂蜜水）
drink some water after lunch（午饭后喝水）
drink a glass of warm milk（喝一杯温牛奶）

文章主旨
张家成员各有不同的健康饮品习惯，如盐水助消化、牛奶助眠，体现家庭对健康生活的重视。
