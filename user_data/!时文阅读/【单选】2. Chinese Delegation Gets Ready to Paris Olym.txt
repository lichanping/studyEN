【单选】2. Chinese Delegation Gets Ready to Paris Olympic Games
【单选】2. 中国代表团准备参加巴黎奥运会。

China will send 405 athletes, including 42 Olympic champions, to the upcoming Paris Olympic Games as a 716-member delegation was officially announced (宣布) in Beijing on Saturday.

中国将派出包括 42 位奥运冠军在内的 405 名运动员，组成一个 716 人的代表团参加即将到来的巴黎奥运会。这一消息已于周六在北京正式宣布。

The delegation (代表团) includes 136 male and 269 female athletes, competing in 30 sports at the Games to be officially opened on July 26.

该代表团包括 136 名男运动员和 269 名女运动员，将参加 7 月 26 日正式开幕的奥运会 30 个项目的比赛。

Zhou Jinqiang, deputy director of China's State General Administration of Sport, announced the delegation list, adding that the average age of the competitors is 25, while 223 athletes will make their Olympic debut in Paris.

中国国家体育总局副局长周进强公布了代表团名单，并补充说，参赛选手平均年龄为 25 岁，而 223 名运动员将在巴黎首次亮相参加奥运会。

Women's skateboarding player Zheng Haohao, 11, is the youngest member of the delegation, while the 37-year-old race walker Liu Hong, who is on her fifth Olympic trip, will be the oldest athlete.

女子滑板运动员郑好好，11 岁，是代表团中最年轻的成员。而 37 岁的竞走运动员刘虹，这是她第五次参加奥运会，将是年龄最大的运动员。

Over 10,000 athletes from more than 200 countries and regions are expected to take part in the Paris Olympics from July 26 to August 11. China's delegation for the Paris Olympic Games arrived in the French capital on Monday evening.

预计来自 200 多个国家和地区的 10000 多名运动员将参加 7 月 26 日至 8 月 11 日的巴黎奥运会。中国参加巴黎奥运会的代表团已于周一晚上抵达法国首都。

Headed by Gao Zhidan, director of China's General Administration of Sport (国家体育总局), a team of Chinese Olympic Committee officials landed at Paris's Charles de Gaulle Airport.

在国家体育总局局长高志丹的率领下，中国奥委会官员团队已抵达巴黎戴高乐机场。

China finished second behind the United States in the medals table at Tokyo 2020 with 38 golds, 32 silvers and 19 bronzes.

在 2020 年东京奥运会上，中国以 38 枚金牌、32 枚银牌和 19 枚铜牌在奖牌榜上位居美国之后，排名第二。
