The Dragon Boat Festival

参考答案1:
The Dragon Boat Festival is one of the most important festivals in China. It is on the fifth day of the fifth lunar month.  On this day,people always have dragon boat races to celebrate it.  What's more,they also make zongzi with family members.  I like the festival because all the family members get together and have a big meal on this day.
