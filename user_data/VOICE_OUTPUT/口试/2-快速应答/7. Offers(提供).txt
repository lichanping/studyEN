7. Offers(提供)
A: Would you like some coffee/tea?
B: Yes,please./No,thanks.
A: Help yourself to some fried chips(薯条).
B: Thank you so much.
A: Can I help you?/What can I do for you?
B: Yes,I'd like a cup of coffee./I'd like a sandwich.
A: Would you like me to help you make some zongzi?
B: Yes,please./No,thanks.
