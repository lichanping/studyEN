How to Keep a Healthy Lifestyle
怎样拥有一个健康的生活方式

What is a healthy lifestyle?Most students may give the answer, healthy food and more exercise. I agree with them, but I am sure that the answer is not enough. Here are some more ways I want to tell you. 

什么是健康的生活方式?大多数学生可能会给出答案，健康的食物和多锻炼。我同意他们的观点，但我确信答案是不够的。这里还有一些我想告诉你的方法。

Get active（积极的）

积极起来

This is the most important way of a healthy lifestyle. Walking, running, swimming and other sports will help you keep fit. 

这是健康生活方式最重要的方式。散步、跑步、游泳和其他运动可以帮助你保持健康。

If your job needs long hours of sitting, get up often and walk. Don't be lazy and it will stop your body and mind. Be active at your workplace, around your neighborhood and wherever possible. 

如果你的工作需要长时间坐着，那就经常站起来走走。不要懒惰，它会停止你的身体和思想。活跃在你的工作场所，你的邻居和任何可能的地方。

Keep clean

保持清洁

Keep the place around you clean. It is another way to make your life healthy. If you live in a dirty place, you may feel sick and get angry easily. 

保持周围环境清洁。这是另一种使你的生活健康的方式。如果你住在一个肮脏的地方，你可能会感到不舒服，很容易生气。

Eat healthily

吃得健康

Food is very important in our daily life. Eating more fruits and vegetables will be a good choice（选择）for you to have a healthy diet. Do not eat junk food, it is not good for your health. 

食物在我们的日常生活中非常重要。多吃水果和蔬菜将是一个很好的选择，让你有一个健康的饮食。不要吃垃圾食品，这对你的健康不好。

Live green

绿色生活

It is the easiest way to live a healthy lifestyle. Plant trees around your house, it not only makes your house beautiful but also makes your feelings good when you live there. What's more, try to save water and stop using things that are bad for the 【environment】. 

这是最简单的健康生活方式。在你的房子周围种植树木，这不仅使你的房子漂亮，也使你的感觉很好，当你住在那里。更重要的是，尽量节约用水，停止使用对环境有害的东西。

If you do like that, our life will be better and better. 

如果你这样做，我们的生活会越来越好。
