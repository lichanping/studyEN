训前准备原文

Mr Green lived in a small town before. He has a job in a big city，so he bought a new flat in the city. And he moved there with his wife and two children last month.
Last weekend，a neighbour came while Mr Green was washing his car. The neighbour stopped and looked at the car.
The neighbour said，"This is a nice car.Is it yours？"
"Sometimes，"answered Mr Green.
The neighbour was surprised. "Sometimes？"he asked. "What do you mean？"
"Well，"answered Mr Green. "When there's a party，it belongs to my daughter，Emily.When there's a football game，it belongs to my son，Tony.When it looks clean，it belongs to my wife，Susan.When it needs cleaning，it belongs to me."
格林先生以前住在一个小镇上。他在一个大城市有份工作，所以在城里买了一套新公寓。上个月，他和妻子以及两个孩子搬到了那里。上周末，格林先生正在洗车时，一位邻居来了。邻居停下来，看着这辆车。邻居说：“这是一辆不错的车。是你的吗？”“有时候是。” 格林先生回答道。邻居感到很惊讶。“有时候？” 他问道。"你是什么意思?"“嗯，” 格林先生回答道。“有派对的时候，车是我女儿埃米莉的。有足球比赛的时候，车是我儿子托尼的。车看起来干净的时候，它是我妻子苏珊的。当车需要清洗的时候，它就是我的。”
