训前准备原文

Long long ago，people in the Noise Kingdom liked making noise. They did everything loudly. The king's birthday was coming. He wanted to hear the loudest noise in the world. So all the people must shout together at twelve o'clock on his birthday! A child said to his parents，"Let's just open our mouths and listen."All the people heard about this idea. At twelve o'clock on the king's birthday，people all opened their mouths and listened. It was so quiet. They heard the songs of the birds and the river. "It's so nice!"said the king. After that，the Noise Kingdom became quiet. People did everything quietly.
很久很久以前，噪音王国的人们喜欢制造噪音。他们做任何事都很大声。国王的生日即将来临。他想听到世界上最响亮的噪音。所以所有人都必须在他生日那天中午 12 点一起大喊！一个孩子对他的父母说：“咱们就张开嘴听吧。” 所有人都听说了这个主意。在国王生日那天中午 12 点，人们都张开嘴听着。四周一片寂静。他们听到了鸟儿的歌声和河流的声音。“这太美妙了！” 国王说。从那以后，噪音王国变得安静了。人们做任何事都是轻声细语的。
