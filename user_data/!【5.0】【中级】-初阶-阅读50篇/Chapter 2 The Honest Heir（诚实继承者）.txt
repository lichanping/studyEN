【1】One day，a rich old businessman meets some children in a small town. He gives them some seeds and says，"Bring me the flowers in three months.The child with the most beautiful flowers will be my heir!"After three months，all the children bring their flowers. Some flowers are big and red. Some flowers are fresh and yellow. Some.. ，but a little boy brings nothing.

【2】He is very sad"Where are your flowers？"The businessman asks him. "I don't know why!"The boy cries and says，"I watered every day.But they don't sprout""Don't cry，good boy.You are honest!""The businessman says，"The seeds are bad and they will never sprout! You will be my heir. "The little boy smiles. He is very happy. The other children feel ashamed.

有一天，一个有钱的老商人在一个小镇上遇到一些孩子。他给了他们一些种子，说：“三个月后把花带来给我。谁的花最漂亮，谁就是我的继承人！”三个月后，所有孩子都带着他们的花来了。有些花又大又红。有些花鲜艳且呈黄色。有些...但是一个小男孩什么也没带。他很伤心。“你的花呢？” 商人问他。“我不知道为什么！” 男孩哭着说，“我每天都浇水。但它们就是不发芽。”“别哭，好孩子。你很诚实！” 商人说，“这些种子是坏的。它们永远不会发芽！”你将成为我的继承人。小男孩笑了。他非常高兴。其他的孩子感到很羞愧。
