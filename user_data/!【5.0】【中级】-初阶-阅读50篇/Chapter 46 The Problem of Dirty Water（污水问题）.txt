The students were having their Chemistry class. Miss Li was telling the students what water was like. After that，she asked her students. "What's water like？"No one answered for a few minutes. Miss Li asked again，"Why don't you answer my question？Didn't I tell you what water is like？"
Just then a student put up his hand and said，"Miss Li，you told us that water has no colour and no smell.However，where to find such kind of water？The water in the river behind my house is always black and it has a bad smell."Most of the students agreed with him.
"I'm sorry，children，"said the teacher. "Our water is getting dirtier and dirtier .That's a problem."
学生们正在上化学课。李老师正在给学生们讲水是什么样的。然后，她向学生们提问。“水是什么样的？” 有几分钟没人回答。李老师又问道：“你们为什么不回答我的问题？我没给你们讲过水是什么样的吗？”就在这时，一个学生举手说：“李老师，您告诉我们水没有颜色也没有气味。然而，到哪里能找到这样的水呢？我家后面河里的水总是黑色的，而且气味很难闻。” 大多数学生都赞同他的观点。“对不起，孩子们，” 老师说。“我们的水越来越脏了。这是个问题。”
