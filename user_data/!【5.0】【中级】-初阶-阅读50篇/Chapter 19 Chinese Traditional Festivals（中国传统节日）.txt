训前准备原文

【1】There are many traditional festivals in China. I like the Spring Festival. It's between January and February. At the Spring Festival，we have a special family dinner，and we eat dumplings，the best thing is that I can get lucky money. My brother likes the Lantern Festival. It is after the Spring Festival. People eat yuanxiao，hang lanterns and do dragon dances. My mother loves the Mid﹣Autumn Festival. She makes delicious moon cakes. My father likes this festival too. He sings songs about the moon. He sings very well.

中国有许多传统节日。我喜欢春节。它在一月和二月之间。在春节，我们会有一顿特别的家庭晚餐，我们吃饺子，最棒的是我能拿到压岁钱。我的哥哥喜欢元宵节。它在春节之后。人们吃元宵、挂灯笼、舞龙。我的妈妈喜欢中秋节。她做美味的月饼。我的爸爸也喜欢这个节日。他会唱关于月亮的歌。他唱歌唱得很好。
