【1】The Spring Festival is our traditional festival.Before the festival，people are busy shopping and cleaning.They buy new clothes and nice food.They decorate their houses with red things.On the Chinese New Year' s Eve，people usually eat dumplings and watch TV.They stay up late to welcome the new year.On the first day of the Chinese New Year，people wear new clothes to visit their relatives and say"Happy New Year" to each other.Children can get lucky money.Everybody is happy that day.In China，years have names.We call it the year of the tiger，the year of the monkey……2024 is the year of the dragon.Do you know the year name of 2025？
春节是我们的传统节日。节前，人们忙着购物和打扫。他们购买新衣服和美味的食物。他们用红色的物品装饰房屋。在除夕夜，人们通常吃饺子、看电视。他们熬夜迎接新年。在大年初一，人们穿上新衣服走亲访友，互道 “新年快乐” 。孩子们可以收到压岁钱。那天每个人都很开心。在中国，年份都有属相。我们称其为虎年、猴年……2024 年是龙年。你知道 2025 年是什么年吗？

第一段段落大意为：
描述春节前的准备工作、除夕夜习俗及拜年活动。

段落短语为：
be busy shopping and cleaning（忙于购物和打扫）
decorate their houses（装饰房子）
stay up late（熬夜）
get lucky money（收压岁钱）
the year of the dragon（龙年）

文章主旨：
春节是中国最重要的传统节日，人们通过大扫除、贴春联、吃年夜饭、守岁和拜年等活动庆祝新年，传递祝福与欢乐。
