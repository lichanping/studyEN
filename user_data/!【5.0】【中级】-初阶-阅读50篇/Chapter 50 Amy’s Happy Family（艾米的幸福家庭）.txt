Look at the photo of Amy's family.
This is her father. He's a police officer. He goes to work by bike. He likes doing kung fu and running. So he has a healthy body. He often plays sports with Amy and her brother in the park on the weekend.
This is her mother. She's a teacher. She goes to work by bus. She likes reading books very much. She often reads books to them in the evening. And shopping is her another hobby. She thinks shopping can make her happy.
This is her brother. He is only three years old. He likes flying kites and watching TV. But her mother always says："Don't watch TV，Tom!"
This is Amy. She is a funny girl. She goes to school with her mother. She likes singing and dancing with her brother on the weekend. She often makes her parents and brother laugh and laugh.
This is a happy family. They love each other.
看看艾米的全家福。这是她的父亲。他是一名警察。他骑自行车去上班。他喜欢练功夫和跑步。所以他有一副健康的体魄。他经常在周末和埃米还有她哥哥在公园里做运动。这是她的母亲。她是一名教师。她乘公交车去上班。她非常喜欢读书。她经常在晚上给他们读书听。购物是她的另一个爱好。她觉得购物能让她开心。这是她的弟弟。他只有三岁。他喜欢放风筝和看电视。但她妈妈总是说:"不要看电视,汤姆!"这是艾米。她是一个有趣的女孩。她和妈妈一起去上学。她喜欢在周末和弟弟一起唱歌跳舞。她经常逗得父母和哥哥弟弟大笑。这是一个幸福的家庭。他们彼此相爱。
