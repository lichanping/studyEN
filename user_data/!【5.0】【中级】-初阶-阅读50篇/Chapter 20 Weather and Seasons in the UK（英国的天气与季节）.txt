训前准备原文

【1】In the U.K.，winter is not very cold and summer is not very hot. There is not a great difference between summer and winter.

【2】The U.K.has a warm winter and a cool summer，because it is an island country. In summer，the sea is cool. The wind from the sea brings cool air to the U.K..

【3】The western wind blows  over the U.K.all year round. It blows from the southwest，and it is the wet wind. It brings rain to the U.K.throughout  the year. The U.K.has a lot of rain all year round.

【4】The four seasons are each three months long. Winter is in December，January and February. Spring is in March，April and May.Summer is in June，July and August. Autumn is in September，October and November.

在英国，冬天不是很冷，夏天也不是很热。夏季和冬季之间差异不大。英国有温暖的冬天和凉爽的夏天，因为它是一个岛国。夏天，海水很凉爽风从大海吹来 ，给英国带来凉爽的空气。西风常年吹拂着英国。它从西南方向吹来，是湿润的风。它一年到头都给英国带来降雨。英国全年降雨丰富。一年四季，每个季节时长为三个月。冬季是12月、1月和2月。春季是3月、4月和5月。 夏天是6月、7月和8月。秋季是 9 月、10 月和 11 月。
