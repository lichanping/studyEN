训前准备原文

【1】Different people like different food. For my family，we always have some meat，vegetables，fruit and eggs every day，because we all think they are good for our health.

【2】My grandpa likes to eat noodles and my grandma likes dumplings. My father thinks fish is very delicious. He often eats it. My mother likes eating salad. Salad is beneficial and healthy. My sister doesn't like meat but chicken is OK. I like beef best，because it tastes good and makes me strong.

【3】Can you share your family's favourite food with me？

不同的人喜欢不同的食物。对我们家来说，我们每天都会吃些肉、蔬菜、水果和鸡蛋，因为我们都认为这些对健康有益。我爷爷喜欢吃面条，我奶奶喜欢吃饺子。我爸爸觉得鱼很好吃。他经常吃鱼。我妈妈喜欢吃沙拉。沙拉有益且健康。我妹妹不喜欢吃肉，但能接受鸡肉。我最喜欢牛肉，因为它味道好，还能让我身体强壮。你能和我分享你家人最喜欢的食物吗?
