训前准备原文

There are many traditional festivals in China. When autumn comes，it's time for the Mid﹣Autumn Festival. There is a festival coming after that. It falls on the ninth day of the ninth month of the lunar calendar. So it is named the double Ninth Festival.
On this day，people usually climb to high places，eat Chongyang cake，drink chrysanthemum wine and wear cornel twigs. They enjoy the beautiful autumn with their family.
This is also a day for Chinese people to show care and love to the elderly. The busy kids come back to visit their lonely elders. There are also some students going to visit the elderly in nursing homes.
中国有许多传统节日。秋天到来时，就到了中秋节。在那之后有一个节日即将来临。它在农历九月初九。所以它被称为重阳节。在这一天，人们通常登高、吃重阳糕、饮菊花酒、插茱萸。他们和家人一起享受美丽的秋景。这也是中国人向老年人表达关爱和敬意的日子。忙碌的孩子们回来探望孤独的长辈。也有一些学生前往养老院看望老人。
