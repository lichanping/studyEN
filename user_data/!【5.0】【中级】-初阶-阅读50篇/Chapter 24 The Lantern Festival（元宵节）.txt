训前准备原文

The Lantern Festival is a Chinese traditional festival. It is on the fifteenth day of the first month in the Chinese calendar. People eat yuanxiao on the Lantern Festival. It's round，which represents family reunion and happiness. There are lantern fairs in many places during the Lantern Festival. Lanterns are made in the shape of different things，such as animals，flowers and so on. People usually write riddles on the lanterns. Many people go to the lantern fairs to enjoy the lanterns and guess the riddles at night. Some children love to carry lanterns and play outside. Sometimes there are some wonderful folk performances，Dragon dance and Yangko. Everything is very interesting and everyone is very happy.
元宵节是中国的传统节日。它在农历正月十五。人们在元宵节吃元宵。元宵是圆的，象征着家庭团圆和幸福。元宵节期间，许多地方都有灯会。灯笼被做成各种形状，比如动物、花卉等等。人们通常在灯笼上写谜语。许多人会在晚上去灯会赏灯猜谜。一些孩子喜欢提着灯笼在外面玩耍。有时会有精彩的民俗表演，舞龙和秧歌。一切都十分有趣，每个人都很开心。
