This is Willow Primary School in the UK. John is a student in the school. He's very helpful. He is funny，too. His favourite class is maths. Miss Smith is John's maths teacher. She is young and kind. Mr.Brown is his science teacher. John likes him best. Today is Tuesday. They have green beans，beef and sandwiches for lunch. They have juice and apples，too. John likes sandwiches best. So Tuesday is John's favourite day.
这是英国的柳树小学。约翰是这所学校的一名学生。他很乐于助人。他也很风趣。他最喜欢的课是数学课。史密斯小姐是约翰的数学老师。她年轻又善良。布朗先生是他的科学老师。约翰最喜欢他。今天是星期二。他们午餐吃青豆、牛肉和三明治。他们也喝了果汁、吃了苹果。约翰最喜欢三明治了。所以星期二是约翰最喜欢的一天。
