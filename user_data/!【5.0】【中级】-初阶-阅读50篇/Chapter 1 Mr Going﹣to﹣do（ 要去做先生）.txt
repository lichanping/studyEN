训前准备原文

【1】People always call Mr Brown "Mr Going﹣to﹣do". Do you know why？ Because Mr Brown always says he is going to do something，but he never does it.

【2】Mr Smith lives next to Mr Brown. Every Saturday，Mr Brown talks to his neighbour Mr Smith. He always says he is going to do something.

【3】He says，"I'm going to clean my house today ."or" I'm going to wash my car tomorrow ."or "I'm going to clean the garden next week."

【4】Mr Smith knows his neighbour is not going to clean his house，or wash his car，or clean his garden. Then he says，"Excuse me，Mr Brown.I'm going to do some work in my house."

【5】Mr Smith often says to his son Dick，"Are you going to do something？Then do it.Don't be another 'Mr Going﹣to﹣do'."

人们总是称布朗先生为"要去做先生"。你知道为什么吗？因为布朗先生总是说他要去做某事，但他从来都不付诸行动。史密斯先生住在布朗先生隔壁。每个星期六，布朗先生都会和邻居史密斯先生聊天。他总是说自己要去做些什么。他说：“我今天要打扫房子。” 或者 “我明天要洗车。” 又或者 “我下周要打扫花园。”史密斯先生知道他的邻居不会打扫房子、洗车，也不会清理花园。然后他说：“不好意思，布朗先生。我要在家干点活儿。”
史密斯先生经常对他儿子迪克说：“你打算做什么事吗？那就去做。别成为另一个‘打算做先生’。”
