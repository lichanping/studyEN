It's Sunday today. Linda is very busy. In the morning，she cleans the room. After breakfast，she plays sports in the park. Then she reads English books at home. After that，she goes to have a drawing class. After lunch，she helps her mother wash the bowls and clean the floor.
In the afternoon，she goes shopping with her parents. They go to a bookshop. Linda buys some storybooks. Because she likes reading stories. In the evening，she watches TV for half an hour. She likes watching TV very much. Her favourite show is cartoons，because she thinks that cartoons are funny. It is a busy day，but Linda thinks it is a good day.
今天是星期天。琳达很忙。早上她打扫房间。早餐后,她在公园里玩运动。然后，她在家读英语书。之后，她去上绘画课。午饭后，她帮妈妈洗碗、擦地板。下午，她和父母去购物。他们去了一家书店。琳达买了些故事书。因为她喜欢读故事。晚上,她看电视半小时。她很喜欢看电视。她最喜欢的节目是动画片，因为她觉得动画片很有趣。这是忙碌的一天，但琳达觉得这是美好的一天。
