训前准备原文

【1】Hello，my name is Bruce. I'm an English boy. I'm a pupil. But I'm in China now because my parents work in China. I like sports very much. I can play football and basketball well. But I can't sing well. I have got two good friends. They are Li Lei and Han Mei. They like sports，too. Li Lei can run fast，but he can't jump far. He can play basketball well，but he can't play football at all. Han Mei is not good at basketball. But she can play football very well. And she can play table tennis. That's her favourite sport.

你好，我叫布鲁斯。我是一个英国男孩。我是一名小学生。但我现在在中国，因为我的父母在中国工作。我非常喜欢运动。我足球和篮球都能打得很好。但我唱歌不太好。我有两个好朋友。他们是李雷和韩梅。他们也喜欢运动。李雷跑得很快，但他跳得不远。他篮球打得很好，但他根本不会踢足球。韩梅不擅长篮球。但她足球踢得很好。而且她会打乒乓球。那是她最喜欢的运动。
