训前准备原文

I'm Chen Jie. I'm a pupil. Our school is big and beautiful. There is a big playground in our school. And there are many flowers in front of the playground. Near the playground ，there is a library. I often read books with my friends there. I have three friends. They are Wu Li，Wu Binbin and Li Hua. They are very helpful. Wu Li is clever. She can speak English well. She often helps us with our English. Wu Binbin is funny. He can draw cartoons well. He often draws cartoons for us. Li Hua is healthy and strong. He can play basketball well. What can I do？ I can play the pipa.
我是陈洁我是一名小学生。我们的学校又大又漂亮。我们学校里有个大操场。在操场前面有很多花。在操场附近,有一个图书馆。我经常和朋友们在那里看书。我有三个朋友。他们是吴力、吴彬彬和李华。他们非常乐于助人。吴力很聪明。她英语说得很好。她经常帮助我们学习英语。吴彬彬很有趣。他卡通画画得很好。他经常为我们画漫画。李华健康又强壮。他打篮球打得很好。我能做什么呢？我会弹琵琶。
