Sandy White is my English teacher. She has big eyes and short brown hair. She's friendly and quiet. She likes Chinese food. She likes eating noodles，so she can use chopsticks well. She loves animals，too. She likes South China Tigers very much. Look，she's looking at the tigers. A big tiger is eating chicken. Two baby tigers are playing with each other. They are so happy.


桑迪·怀特是我的英语老师。她有一双大眼睛和棕色的短发。她友好且文静。她喜欢中国菜。她喜欢吃面条，所以她用筷子用得很熟练。她也喜欢动物。她非常喜欢华南虎。看，她正在看老虎。一只大老虎正在吃鸡肉。两只小虎崽在互相玩耍。他们很开心。
