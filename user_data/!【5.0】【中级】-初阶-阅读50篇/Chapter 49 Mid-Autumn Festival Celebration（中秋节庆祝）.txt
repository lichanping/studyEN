训前准备原文

Today is the Mid﹣Autumn Festival. In the evening，we have a big family dinner. Look at the food on the table. There is chicken，fruit，vegetables. They look delicious. We drink some juice. We sit beside the table and say，"Cheers! Happy Mid﹣Autumn Festival!"We give our best wishes to each other. At night，the moon is round and bright. It looks like a ball. We enjoy the moon. We eat moon cakes too. My family and I are very happy.
今天是中秋节。晚上，我们有一顿丰盛的家庭晚餐。看看桌子上的食物。有鸡肉、水果、蔬菜。它们看起来很美味。我们喝了点果汁。我们坐在桌旁，说：“干杯！中秋快乐！” 我们互相致以最美好的祝愿。夜晚，月亮又圆又亮。它看起来像个球。我们赏月。我们也吃月饼。我和我的家人非常开心。
