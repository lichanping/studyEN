Hi! My name's Sue. Welcome to my home. I have two bedrooms，a living room，a kitchen，a study and a bathroom. In my bedroom，I have a small bed. In the living room，I have a chair，a desk and a TV. In the study，I have a computer and a desk. I like my room.
Come and meet my family! My family has four people. This is my father. He's a teacher. He's tall. He likes sports. This is my mother. She's a teacher，too. She likes English. This is my brother，Bob. He's a basketball player. He's strong. I love my family.
And I have a friend. She is Mary. She is tall and thin. She has big eyes and a small red mouth. She has long black hair. She is a good student. We often play together. What about you？
