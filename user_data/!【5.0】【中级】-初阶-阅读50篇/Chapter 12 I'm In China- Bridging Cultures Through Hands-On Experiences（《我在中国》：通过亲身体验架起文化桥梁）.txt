训前准备原文

【1】Bill and Jane are getting on the boat to Qingrendao，Hainan. They are joining in a program called I'm In China for one day. It is a new reality show on TV. About 500 people from across the world come to it to have different experiences.

【2】There are many kinds of activities in the program. Chengdu，Sichuan is the first stop of the I'm In China program. The participants can care about pandas' life together with panda keepers. They can learn kung fu at Shaolin Temple in Henan. They can be a high-speed train worker in Wuhan，Hubei. And they can learn how to make hand-pulled noodles in Lanzhou，Gansu. Taking care of pandas in Chengdu is the most popular activity. In fact，for most foreigners，one of their biggest wishes is to see pandas before coming to China.

【3】I'm In China opens a new door for the world to China. With warm heart，the program welcomes foreign friends to see China by themselves. By joining it，they can find out what everyday life is like in China. Through the program，the outside world can know more about China. The workers of the program will choose the best from those 500 people. These best people will work as ambassadors to share their real experiences of Chinese culture with the world. They are like a bridge between China and the world.

比尔和简正要登上前往海南情人岛的船。他们将参加一个名为《我在中国》的一日节这是一档新的电视真人秀节目。来自世界各地的约 500 人参与其中，体验不同的生活。节目中有各种各样的活动。四川成都是《我在中国》节目的第一站。参与者可以和熊猫饲养员一起关注熊猫的生活。他们可以在河南少林寺学习功夫。他们可以在湖北武汉体验当高铁工作人员。他们还可以在甘肃兰州学习如何制作拉面。在成都照顾熊猫是最受欢迎的活动。事实上，对大多数外国人来说，来中国之前能看到熊猫是他们最大的愿望之一。《我在中国》为世界打开了一扇了解中国的新窗口。该节目热情欢迎外国友人亲自来感受中国。通过参与节目，他们能了解中国的日常生活是什么样的。通过这个节目，外部世界能够更多地了解中国。节目工作人员将从这 500 人中选出最优秀的。这些最优秀的人将担任文化大使，与世界分享他们对中国文化的真实体验。他们就像中国与世界之间的桥梁。
