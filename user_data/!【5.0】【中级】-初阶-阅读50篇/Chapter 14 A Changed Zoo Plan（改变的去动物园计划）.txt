【1】Today is Sunday. Sarah and Sam get up very early，and they are very happy. Because Mum and Dad will take them to the zoo.

【2】When their Dad calls them，Sarah and Sam are discussing how to go to the zoo. Dad says"Your Mum is ill.She should see a doctor this morning，so we can not go to the zoo today."Sarah and Sam are very sad and worried. Sarah asks"Does Mum catch a cold？""Mum has a fever."Dad says. "How does Mum feel now？"Sam asks. Dad says to him"Mum is not well.We should go to the hospital now."

今天是周日。萨拉和山姆早早起床，他们非常高兴。因为爸妈要带他们去动物园。当爸爸给他们打电话时，萨拉和山姆正在讨论如何去动物园。爸爸说：“你们的妈妈生病了。她今天早上得去看医生，所以我们今天不能去动物园了。” 萨拉和山姆非常难过和担心。萨拉问：“妈妈感冒了吗？” 爸爸说：“妈妈发烧了。”“妈妈现在感觉怎么样？” 山姆问。爸爸对他说：“妈妈不舒服。我们现在得去医院。”
