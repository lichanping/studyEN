One morning a fox sees a rooster. He thinks，"This is my breakfast."He comes up to the rooster and says，"I know you can sing.Can you sing for me？"The rooster is happy. He closes his eyes and sings. The fox catches him in his mouth. The people in the field see the fox. They shout，"Look，look!The fox is carrying the rooster away."The rooster says to the fox，"Mr Fox，the people say you are carrying their rooster away.Tell them I'm yours.Not theirs."The fox opens his mouth and says，"The rooster is mine，not yours."Just then the rooster runs away and jumps onto the tree.


一天早上，一只狐狸看到了一只公鸡。他心想：“这就是我的早餐。” 他走到公鸡面前说：“我知道你会唱歌。你能为我唱一首吗？” 公鸡很高兴。他闭上眼睛开始唱歌。狐狸一口把他叼住了。田地里的人看到了狐狸。他们大喊：“看，看！狐狸把公鸡叼走了。” 公鸡对狐狸说：“狐狸先生，人们说你把他们的公鸡叼走了。告诉他们我是你的，不是他们的。” 狐狸张开嘴说：“这只公鸡是我的，不是你们的。” 然后公鸡逃走了，跳到了一棵树上。
