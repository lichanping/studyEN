训前准备原文

I am looking for a pen pal. My name is Jacket. I live in the USA. I am 12 years old. I am tall and thin. I have many hobbies. I like Chinese food very much. I like singing，dancing and making friends. I often go hiking on the weekend. My father is a doctor. He often goes to China by plane. He often tells me some stories about China. He likes doing kungfu. I want to study Chinese. Do you want to be my pen pal？ Write tome，please.


我正在寻找一位笔友。我的名字是杰克。我住在美国。我十二岁了。我又高又瘦。我有很多爱好。我非常喜欢中国菜。我喜欢唱歌、跳舞和交朋友。我周末经常去远足。我的父亲是一名医生。他经常乘飞机去中国。他经常给我讲一些关于中国的故事。他喜欢练功夫。我想学中文。你想成为我的笔友吗？请给我写信。
