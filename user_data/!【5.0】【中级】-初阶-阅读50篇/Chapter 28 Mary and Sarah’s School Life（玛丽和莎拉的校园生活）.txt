Hi，I'm Mary. I have a good friend. Her name is Sarah. She is clever. We are in the same class. We go to school from Monday to Friday. We have four classes in the morning and two in the afternoon every weekday. I like Mondays，because we have science on Mondays. I can make a robot in the science class. Sarah likes Thursdays，because we have maths on Thursdays. She likes maths classes. Our math teacher is Mr Li. He is very knowledgeable. We have lunch at school. My favourite food is salad. Sarah likes hot dogs best. We both like the weekend. Because we can play the piano together on the weekend.
