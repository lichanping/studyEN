训前准备原文

There are many different jobs，for example，doctor，scientist，postman，pilot and so on. If you work hard，you can do them well.
Chen Hua is a writer. She writes books for children，so she often goes to the library and reads storybooks. She works in a newspaper office in Beijing. She goes to work by car. She works hard，and many people like to read her books.
Tom，a police officer，likes helping people. He likes listening to music. He often helps people find their way. He goes to work by subway.
Hu Ming is a postman. He works in a post office. He likes singing. He likes Liu Huan best. Where does he live？ Oh，he lives in Hong Kong. And he goes to work by subway.
有许多不同的职业，例如医生、科学家、邮递员、飞行员等等。如果你努力工作，你就能把这些工作做好。陈华是一名作家。她为儿童写书，所以她经常去图书馆读故事书。她在北京的一家报社工作。她开车去上班。她工作努力，很多人喜欢读她写的书。汤姆是一名警察，他喜欢帮助别人。他喜欢听音乐。他经常帮助人们找路。他乘地铁去上班。胡明是一名邮递员。他在邮局工作。他喜欢唱歌。他最喜欢刘欢。他住在哪里？哦,他住在香港。他乘地铁去上班。
