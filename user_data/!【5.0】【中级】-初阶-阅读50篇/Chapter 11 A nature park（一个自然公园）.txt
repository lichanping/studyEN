训前准备原文

【1】There is a nature park near my house. The nature park is so quiet! In the park there is a high mountain. In front of the mountain there is a small village. There is a lake near the village. There are many ducks on the lake. They can swim on the lake. There are some trees in the village. They are tall and green.

【2】This nature park is beautiful. Look! There is a big white bird in the sky! And there are some animals in the nature park，too. They are cute and lovely. I like this nature park.

我家附近有一个自然公园。这个自然公园十分静谧！公园里有一座高山。山前有个小村庄。村子附近有个湖。湖面上有许多鸭子。它们能在湖里游泳。村子里有一些树。这些树又高又绿。这个自然公园很美。看! 天空里有一只大白鸟! 自然公园里也有一些动物。它们既可爱又迷人。我喜欢这个自然公园。
