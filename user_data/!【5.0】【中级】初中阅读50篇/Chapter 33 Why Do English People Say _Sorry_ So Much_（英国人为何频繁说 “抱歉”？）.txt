【1】People like to say sorry in the UK.If a person burps at the dinner table or he is late for a meeting，you'll probably hear him saying "Oh，I'm so sorry!"
【2】But why do the English people say sorry so much？A US scientist Robert Trivers gave a possible answer in his studies.He says that we are polite because we need to be a part of a group and join in the society.If we want to be welcome and safe，we need to follow the rules，help each other and say sorry.
【3】As an English magazine says，in the UK，being polite could be "a symbol of the English class system in the old time".Sometimes people say sorry to show they are in a high class.Sometimes，they say sorry to show respect to others.And other times they may just say sorry to stay away from trouble.
【4】Does this mean that when English people say sorry，they are not showing they did something wrong？The answer may be "yes" sometimes.But saying sorry is useful.If you're in the UK，please remember，a little "sorry" can help you to go a long way.
在英国，人们喜欢说 “对不起” 。如果一个人在餐桌上打嗝或者开会迟到了，你可能会听到他说：“哦，我很抱歉！”但为什么英国人这么频繁地说 “对不起” 呢？一位美国科学家罗伯特・特里弗斯在他的研究中给出了一个可能的答案。他说我们有礼貌是因为我们需要成为群体的一部分并融入社会。如果我们想要受到欢迎并保证安全，我们就需要遵守规则、互相帮助并说 “对不起” 。	正如一本英国杂志所说，在英国，礼貌可能是 “英国阶级制度的一种象征” 。有时人们说 “对不起” 是为了显示自己属于上流阶层。有时，他们说 “对不起” 是为了表示对他人的尊重。还有些时候，他们说 “对不起” 可能只是为了避免麻烦。	这是否意味着当英国人说“对不起”时，他们并不是在表明自己做错了什么？	答案有时可能是 “是的” 。但说 “对不起” 是有用的。如果你在英国，请记住，一句小小的 “对不起” 能帮你走得更远。
