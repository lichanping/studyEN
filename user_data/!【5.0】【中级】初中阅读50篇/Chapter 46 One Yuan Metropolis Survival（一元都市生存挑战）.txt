【1】If you are given only 1 yuan，could you live in a city for one day？It seems that this may be a little difficult.But students from Xi'an did it.
【2】n April 10.more than 60 students from the Middle School Attached to North.western Polytechnical University took part in the One Yuan Metropolis Survival，The charity organization for teenagers called Imperceptible Education held this activity.Students not only had to live，but they also had to deal with a lot of hard tasks.
【3】To make money，many students looked for part﹣time jobs and saw how hard life was.Zhang Qiyue，14.asked more than 10 restaurants for a job and finally got one chance."We were upset when they said no.But slowly，we were used to it. "he said.After being a waiter for an hour，he got 25 yuan.
【4】Some made use of their talents by singing and dancing in parks or shopping malls.Many people who walked by took notice and helped them.Zhao Jiacheng.14.drew pictures and made papercrafts."This showed it's important to learn a special skill.he said.
【5】Living was hard，but finishing the tasks wasn't any easier.They went to different places to finish tasks in a short time.The most amazing one was when they had to exchange things worth thousands of yuan with only a piece of paper in half an hour."We learnt how to persuade others.From balloon to cake to a bottle of yogurt…after almost 20 tries，we got an expensive necklace，" Zhao Jiacheng said.
如果只给你一元钱，你能在城市里生活一天吗？这似乎有点困难。但西安的学生做到了。4 月 10 日。西北工业大学附属中学的 60 多名学生参加了“一元城市生存挑战”。这个针对青少年的慈善组织“无痕教育”举办了此次活动。学生们不仅要维持生活，还得应对许多艰巨的任务。为了赚钱，许多学生找兼职工作，体会到了生活的艰辛。14 岁的张奇 Yue 向十多家餐馆求职，最终得到了一个机会。“他们拒绝时我们很沮丧，但慢慢地，我们习惯了。” 他说。做了一个小时的服务员后，他挣了 25 元。一些学生在公园或购物中心唱歌跳舞，发挥自己的才艺。许多路人注意到并帮助了他们。14 岁的赵家成画画、制作纸工艺品。“这表明学习一项特殊技能很重要。” 他说。生活很艰难，完成任务也并不轻松。他们要去不同的地方，在短时间内完成任务。最令人惊叹的是，他们得在半小时内用一张纸去交换价值数千元的物品。“我们学会了如何说服别人。从气球到蛋糕再到一瓶酸奶……经过差不多二十次尝试，我们得到了一条昂贵的项链。”赵家诚说。
