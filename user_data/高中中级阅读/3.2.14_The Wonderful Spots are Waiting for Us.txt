Dashing Scotland

雄纠纠的苏格兰

Head to Edinburgh, the capital of Scotland, to explore key historical sights – from the Royal Mile to Edinburgh Castle.Then, breathe in the fresh air of Loch Lomond.Rest your tired legs back at a comfortable hotel in the centre of Edinburgh.The four-day journey is the first choice for senior citizens.

前往苏格兰首府爱丁堡，探索从皇家一英里到爱丁堡城堡的重要历史景点。然后，呼吸一下洛蒙德湖的新鲜空气。在爱丁堡市中心的一家舒适的酒店休息你疲惫的双腿。为期四天的旅程是老年人的首选。

The Real Costa Rica

真正的哥斯达黎加

Explore a Costa Rican cloud forest, surf the best Pacific waves and sleep near the Arenal volcano.This eight-day adventure is perfect for wildlife watchers and adventure lovers — you'll spot various wild animals, and white-water rafting through the jungle.

探索哥斯达黎加的云雾森林，在最棒的太平洋海浪中冲浪，在阿雷纳尔火山附近睡觉。这个为期八天的冒险活动非常适合野生动物观察者和冒险爱好者——您将看到各种野生动物，并在丛林中漂流。

Discover Jordan

发现乔丹

Discover a lesser-known side of Jordan.On this nine-day journey, you'll hike through brown-coloured mountains, dive in the Red Sea and rest in wild camp under the stars.But not before the chance to sample street food, taste local wines and explore the old city ruins in Amman.Eager adventurers, this trip is made for you.

发现约旦鲜为人知的一面。在这9天的旅程中，您将徒步穿越棕色的山脉，在红海潜水，在星空下的野外营地休息。但在此之前，你将有机会品尝街头小吃，品尝当地葡萄酒，探索安曼的旧城遗址。热情的冒险家们，这次旅行就是为你们准备的。

Speedy Greece

快速的希腊

With ancient temples, antiquity-stuffed museums and more theatres than any other city in the world, when it comes to culture, Athens is on top of it.

雅典拥有古老的庙宇，充满古董的博物馆，比世界上任何其他城市都多的剧院，说到文化，雅典是首屈一指的。

But what you may not know is that it also has one of the most lively street art scenes in Europe, which you'll explore in the company of our Local Insider.

但你可能不知道的是，它也有欧洲最热闹的街头艺术场景之一，你将在我们的本地人的陪同下去探索。

End this extraordinary five-day adventure with a sunset wine-tasting session.
