The Big Year of China
中国体育大年

Next year will be a big year for sports in China. It will host (举办) the 24th Winter Olympics in Beijing, and it will host the 19th Asian Games in Hangzhou. Hangzhou is the third Chinese city to host the Asian Games after Beijing and Guangzhou in 1990 and 2010. 

明年将是中国体育的大年。它将在北京举办第24届冬奥会，并将在杭州举办第19届亚运会。杭州是继1990年的北京和2010年的广州之后第三个举办过亚运会的中国城市。

The first Asian Games held by China was in Beijing in 1990. It also means a lot to China. The Beijing Asian Games was the first large international sports event (国际体育赛事) hosted by China. To make the Games a great success, Chinese people worked hard to get ready for it. Beijing built a lot of roads and gyms (体育馆) like Beijing Olympic Sports Center and Asian Games Village. The Games helped the world see China. 

中国于1990年在北京举办了第一届亚运会。这对中国也意义重大。北京亚运会是中国举办的第一届大型国际体育赛事。为了使亚运会取得成功，中国人民努力工作，为它做准备。北京修建了许多道路和体育馆，如北京奥林匹克体育中心和亚运村。亚运会让世界看到了中国。

For the 2022 Asian Games, Hangzhou has started to build 33 sports venues (场馆). The themes of the Hangzhou Asian Games are “green, smart, economical and civilized (节俭和文明)”. 

为迎接2022年亚运会，杭州已开始建设33个体育场馆。杭州亚运会的主题是“绿色、智慧、经济、文明”。
