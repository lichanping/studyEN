A Great Place — The Black Forest
一个很棒的地方 — 黑森林

Do you remember when your grandma told you the story of Snow White?Ah, the happy days of childhood!

你还记得你奶奶给你讲的白雪公主的故事吗?啊，童年的快乐时光!

But did you know that Snow White comes from Germany?It's just one story from the 209 in Grimm's Fairy Tales (《格林童话》). The Grimm brothers started to collect fairy tales in 1806. Their first book came out in 1812. 

但是你知道白雪公主来自德国吗?这只是格林童话中209个故事中的一个。格林兄弟从1806年开始收集童话故事。他们的第一本书出版于1812年。

Why are German fairy tales so interesting?Maybe it's because they come from a great place famous for its stories—the Black Forest. 

为什么德国童话如此有趣?也许是因为他们来自一个以故事闻名的地方——黑森林。

The Black Forest is in southwest Germany. It's the largest forest in the country—and one of the most beautiful. It's famous for its trees and lovely views. There are valleys and waterfalls there. It's a good place to start a story. 

黑森林位于德国西南部。它是全国最大的森林，也是最美丽的森林之一。它以它的树木和美丽的景色而闻名。那里有山谷和瀑布。这是一个开始故事的好地方。

Don't forget to bring something back if you visit it. People there are good at making clocks, musical instruments and watches. 

如果你去参观，别忘了带点东西回来。那里的人擅长制作钟表、乐器和手表。
