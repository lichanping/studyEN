The Police can Help us Anytime
警察随时都会帮助我们

Mr. Wang's old grandfather lives with him. Every morning, he goes out for a walk in the garden and comes home at 12:30 for lunch.

王先生的老祖父和他住在一起。每天早上，他出去在花园里散步，然后在12:30回家吃午饭。

But one morning, a police car stops outside Mr.Wang's house at twelve, and two police-men help his grandfather to get out. One of them says to Mr.Wang: “The old man loses his way (迷路) home in the garden and calls us for help, so we drive the car to bring him home. ”Mr. Wang is very surprised but he thanks the policemen and then they leave. “But Grandfather,” he asks, “You go to that garden every day for twenty years.  How can you lose your way home?”The old man smiles and says: “I can’t lose my way but I’m just tired and don’t want to go home on foot. ”

但是一天早上，一辆警车停在王先生的家门口，在两名警察的帮助下，他的祖父下了车。其中一人对他说：“这位老人在花园里迷路了，打电话给我们求助，所以我们开车把他带回家。”王先生很惊讶，但他感谢了警察，然后他们离开了。“但是爷爷，”他问，“20年来您每天都去那个花园。您怎么会迷路呢?”老人笑着说:“我不会迷路的，但我只是累了，不想步行回家。”
