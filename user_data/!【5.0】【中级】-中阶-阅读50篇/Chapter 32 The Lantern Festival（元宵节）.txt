【1】The Lantern Festival ，with a history of over 2，000 years，falls on the 15th day of the 1st lunar month.It marks the last day of the Spring Festival.And it's a very important festival for Chinese people.There are all kinds of traditional activities during the Lantern Festival.
【2】On the day of the festival，lanterns of different shapes and sizes are seen everywhere.People can not only enjoy the beauty of lanterns，but guess lantern riddles （灯谜）.Eating yuanxiao or tangyuan is a must﹣do on the day.The ball﹣shaped sweet carries Chinese people's good wish for their family over the following days.During the Lantern Festival，there are also many lantern fairs for people to join in the fun in public places.
元宵节有两千多年的历史，在农历正月十五。它标志着春节的最后一天。这对中国人来说是一个非常重要的节日。元宵节期间有各种各样的传统活动。节日当天，各种形状和大小的灯笼随处可见。人们不仅可以欣赏灯笼之美，还能猜灯谜。这一天吃元宵或汤圆是必做之事。这种球形甜食承载着中国人对家人未来日子的美好祝愿。元宵节期间，还有许多灯会，人们可以在公共场所参与其中，享受乐趣。
