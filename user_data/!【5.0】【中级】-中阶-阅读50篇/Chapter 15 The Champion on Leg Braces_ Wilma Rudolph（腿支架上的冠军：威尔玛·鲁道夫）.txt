【1】Wilma Rudolph was a great American athlete .She was from a large family.There were 22 children in her family.When Wilma Rudolph was four，she got a very serious illness .From then on，she couldn't walk any more.
【2】Wilma's mother decided to do everything she could to help Wilma to walk again.She took Wilma to a hospital to get therapy every week，but it didn't help much.Then she followed the doctors' advice and massaged her daughter's legs every day.Wilma's brothers and sisters also massaged Wilma's legs every day.
【3】With the help of her family，Wilma Rudolph got better and better.When she was eight，she could walk with a leg brace .Three years later，she could play basketball without the leg brace.
【4】Wilma Rudolph started to run in high school.In the 1960 Olympic Games，she won three gold medals.Her experience encouraged many people.
威尔玛・鲁道夫是一位伟大的美国运动员。她来自一个大家庭。她家里有 22 个孩子。威尔玛・鲁道夫四岁时，患上了一种非常严重的疾病。从那以后，她再也无法行走。威尔玛的母亲决定竭尽所能帮助威尔玛重新学会走路。她每周带威尔玛去医院接受治疗，但效果甚微。然后，她听从医生的建议，每天为女儿按摩腿部。威尔玛的兄弟姐妹也每天为她按摩腿部。在家人的帮助下，威尔玛・鲁道夫的状况越来越好。她八岁时，借助腿部支架能够行走。三年后，她不用腿部支架就能打篮球了。威尔玛・鲁道夫在高中时开始跑步。在 1960 年奥运会上，她赢得了三枚金牌。她的经历鼓舞了许多人。

威尔玛·鲁道夫是美国伟大的运动员。她来自一个大家庭，家里有22个孩子。四岁时，她得了重病，从此再也不能走路。

重点语言点：got better and better （ 逐渐康复）
walk with a leg brace （ 借助腿支架行走）
