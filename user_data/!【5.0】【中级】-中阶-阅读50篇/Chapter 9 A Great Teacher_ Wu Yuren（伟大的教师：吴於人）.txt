【1】Do you know Touching China？It is a TV program on CCTV played once a year.It first appeared in 2003.Ten role models are praised every year in the program for their moving stories and great achievements for our society.
【2】Wu Yuren，a 74﹣year﹣old physics teacher from China's Tongji University，was among them.She won the award because she shared popular physics experiments through short videos in 2023.By the end of 2023，over 5.2 million people had watched her short videos.
【3】In her short videos，she often wears a coat with several pockets and takes some "toys".She uses them as teaching tools.She uses the things we may see every day to answer people's physical questions in a simple way.
【4】"I made these videos to make young people curious about common life.And I hope they can use everyday things to find the truth of science，" Wu said. Wu has been making short videos for 6 years. She said children's interest in doing experiments made her keep making videos. According to CCTV，her videos are like "classrooms without walls". She is really a great teacher!

你知道《感动中国》吗？这是中央电视台的一档电视节目，每年播出一次。它于 2003 年首次亮相。每年在这个节目中，十位榜样因其感人的故事和为我们社会做出的巨大成就而受到赞扬。74 岁的吴於人，是来自中国同济大学的一名物理教师，她也是其中之一。2023年，她因通过短视频分享科普物理实验，荣获2022年度感动中国奖。截至 2023 年底，超过 520万人次观看过她的短视频。在她的短视频里，她常常穿着一件有多个口袋的外套，还带着一些 “玩具”。她把这些当作教学工具。她用我们日常可能见到的东西，以通俗易懂的方式解答人们的物理问题。“我制作这些视频是为了激发年轻人对日常生活的好奇心。我希望他们能用日常物品去探寻科学的真相。” 吴老师说道。吴老师制作短视频已有 6 年时间。她说孩子们对做实验的浓厚兴趣促使她不断制作视频。据央视报道，她的视频就如同 “没有围墙的教室”。她真是一位了不起的老师！
