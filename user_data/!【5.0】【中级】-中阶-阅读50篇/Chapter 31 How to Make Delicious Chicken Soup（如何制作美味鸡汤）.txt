【2】Chicken soup is a kind of traditional Chinese food.It is very delicious and it's good for our health.And I think it's easy to cook it.If you follow my steps，you can enjoy the delicious chicken soup.First，you need a saucepan .Clean the saucepan and put eight bowls of water into the saucepan.Heat it as you do other things for cooking.You will need some chicken，carrots，salt and pepper.Cut up the chicken and carrots.When the water boils，put the chicken into the saucepan.Wait for 20 minutes and add the carrots.Simmer for one hour，and then add salt and pepper.Finally，pour out chicken soup into bowls.Don't eat at once.Because it's too hot!You can enjoy it five minutes later.
你喜欢鸡汤吗？你知道如何煮出美味的鸡汤吗？让我来告诉你！鸡汤是一种传统的中国食物。它非常美味，而且对我们的健康有益。我觉得煮鸡汤很容易。如果你按照我的步骤来，就能享受到美味的鸡汤。首先，你需要一个炖锅。清洗平底锅，然后往平底锅里倒入八碗水。在加热水的同时，你可以准备其他烹饪材料。你需要准备一些鸡肉、胡萝卜、盐和胡椒粉。把鸡肉和胡萝卜切碎。当水沸腾时，把鸡肉放入平底锅中。等 20 分钟后加入胡萝卜。小火慢炖一小时，然后加入盐和胡椒粉。最后，把鸡汤盛到碗里。不要马上吃。因为太烫了！五分钟后你就可以享用了。
