Hunt-Broersma
亨特-布罗尔斯玛

When amputee（被截肢者）runner Jacky Hunt-Broersma says her motto is, "I can do hard things," she is not joking. She has completed 104 marathons in 104 days, using a prosthetic（假体的）leg. 

当截肢运动员杰基·亨特-布洛斯玛说她的座右铭是“我能做困难的事情”时，她不是在开玩笑。她使用假肢在104天内完成了104次马拉松比赛。

Hunt-Broersma, 46, finished her 104th marathon on April 30, near her home in Phoenix, Arizona, breaking an unofficial world record along the way. Brick Runners, an organization that supports athletes who raise money for charities, even designed one of her favorite T-shirts, which reads, "Strong Has Many Forms. "

今年4月30日，46岁的亨特-布罗尔斯玛在亚利桑那州凤凰城的家附近完成了她的第104次马拉松比赛，一路上打破了一项非官方的世界纪录。Brick Runners是一个支持运动员为慈善机构筹集资金的组织，该组织甚至设计了一件她最喜欢的t恤，上面写着"坚强有多种形式"。

Hunt-Broersma, who is from South Africa, lost her leg below the knee to cancer when she was 26 years old. 

亨特-布罗尔斯玛来自南非，26岁时因患癌症失去了膝盖以下的腿。

She ran the first of her 104 marathons on January 17, 2022. Initially, 100 in as many days, to beat non-disabled runner Alysa Amos Clark's 100 record of 95. But in March, after non-disabled British runner Kate Jayden unofficially broke Clark's record with 101 marathons in 101 days, Hunt-Broersma knew she'd need to run at least 101. 

2002年1月17日，她完成了104次马拉松比赛中的第一次比赛。最初是100天100次马拉松，为了打破了非残疾运动员阿丽莎·阿莫斯·克拉克在100年创下的95次的纪录。但在今年3月，英国非残疾跑步者凯特·杰登非正式地打破了克拉克的纪录，在101天内跑了101场马拉松，亨特-布罗尔斯玛知道她至少需要跑101场。

As she did her daily 42. 2-kilometer runs, Hunt-Brocrsma's social media popularity grew, and she raised over US $67, 000 to help other amputee runners buy the prosthetic arms and legs they need. These can cost over US $10, 000, and health insurance companies don't usually pay for them. 

就像她每天做的42. 2公里长跑中，亨特-布罗克斯玛在社交媒体上的知名度不断上升，她筹集了超过6. 7万美元，帮助其他截肢跑步者购买他们所需的假肢。这些费用可能超过1万美元，而健康保险公司通常不支付这些费用。

Hunt-Broersma said she hoped to inspire people everywhere to push themselves to do challenging things even if they have disabilities. 

亨特-布罗尔斯玛说，她希望能激励世界各地的人去做有挑战性的事情，即使他们有残疾。

Guinness World Records said it knew about Hunt-Broersma's 104 marathons, and that it would take 12 to 15 weeks to look at the evidence before the record can be confirmed by it. 

吉尼斯世界纪录表示，它知道亨特-布罗尔斯马的104场马拉松比赛，在记录得到证实之前，需要12到15周的时间来查看证据。
